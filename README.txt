THE ACTION GROUP TRACKER  (v16)
================================
The Utah tile now shows both companies.

THE DUAL TILE
-------------
  The Action Utah tile on the hub is sliced corner to corner — top-right
  corner straight down to the bottom-left corner:
    · above the diagonal: Action, black, exactly as before
      ("ACTION UTAH", region line, rep count, Enter)
    · the diagonal itself: a navy stripe in #1e3078 — All Pro's own navy,
      sampled from their logo
    · below the diagonal: WHITE, with the real All Pro Service Group
      logo (pulled from allproservicegroup.com) and "ALL PRO" beneath it

  Why white, not another dark panel: their logo is 73% dark navy and
  disappears on black. White is also faithful to their brand — their
  whole site is navy-on-white — and the black/white split makes
  "two companies, one crew" read instantly from across the room.

  New file: allpro-logo.png (transparent, from their site).

  The tile still enters the same Action Utah location — nothing about
  logins, reps, or data changed. This is the visual answer to the same
  fact the ACTION | ALL PRO call toggle handles in the data: Utah runs
  both.

  The Action text is width-capped so every word stays inside the black
  triangle as it narrows toward the bottom.

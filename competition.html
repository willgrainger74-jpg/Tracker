How the Bonus Works
Four core categories pay 25% to the team that wins and 12.5% for second: Turnover Revenue · Certified Reviews (1 pt review / 2 pts photo review) · Close % (non-runners excludable) · Gym (most sessions). Action Stats (most sold) is a bonus category worth 10% for first and 5% for second. Whichever team wins the most categories takes the crown.
